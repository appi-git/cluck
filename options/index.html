How to read a block

Each block is divided into 9 grids. Each column (set of vertical grids) from left to right corresponds to a power of four.

First Column 4^0 (=1)
Second Column 4^1 (=4)
Third Column 4^2 (=16)

Each of the three grids in a column can either be active or inactive. For each active grid in a column, you add up the corresponding power of four (1, 4 or 16) represented by that column. The total sum is the number represented by the block.

How to read a clock

The clock has two modes, landscape and portrait. The current format is Hours:Minutes (from left to right) in landscape and Hours:Minutes (top to bottom) in portrait.
